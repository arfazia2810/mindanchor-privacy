MindAnchor Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

MindAnchor ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how the app handles your information.

1. Data We Collect

MindAnchor does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information you enter — including drift logs, trigger notes, and settings — is stored exclusively on your device using local storage.

2. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device via Hive (an embedded database). This data never leaves your device. We have no servers, no accounts, and no cloud sync.

3. Permissions

The app may request access to certain device features:

  • Microphone: Used only for audio-related features such as playback or guided exercises. No audio is recorded, stored, or transmitted.

All permissions are optional and used strictly to support app functionality.

4. Third-Party Services

MindAnchor does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

5. Children's Privacy

MindAnchor does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app contains no account creation or data submission features.

6. Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time using Settings → Reset All Data. Uninstalling the app will also remove all locally stored data.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy if the app's functionality changes. Updates will be reflected in new app versions on the App Store.
